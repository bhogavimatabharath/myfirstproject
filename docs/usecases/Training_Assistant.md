# Training Assistant Guide

## Quiz Generation and Content Summarization Using Local LLM

### Quiz Generation
1. **Define the Subject Matter**: Clearly outline the topic for the quiz.
2. **Input Content**: Provide the content to the local LLM related to the subject matter.
3. **Generate Questions**: Use the LLM to create multiple-choice questions or open-ended questions based on the content.
4. **Review and Edit**: Ensure the generated questions are relevant and accurately reflect the content.
5. **Testing**: Share the quiz with a small group to test its effectiveness and make adjustments as necessary.

### Content Summarization
1. **Collect Relevant Documents**: Gather all documents that need summarization.
2. **Input Data**: Feed the documents into the local LLM.
3. **Generate Summaries**: Instruct the LLM to provide concise summaries of the collected documents.
4. **Review Summaries**: Check the summaries for accuracy and clarity.
5. **Usage**: Utilize the summaries for training modules or reference materials.
